Hon. Kenneth Gilbert Adjei
Minister

Hon. Kenneth Gilbert Adjei is a Ghanaian politician currently serving as the Minister for Works, Housing and Water Resources under the administration of President John Dramani Mahama as of 2026. He was appointed to this role in February 2025.

Born on September 20, 1978, he hails from Trede in the Ashanti Region of Ghana.

An alumnus of Opoku Ware School, where he notably served as the Dining Hall Prefect.
